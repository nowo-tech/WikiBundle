Wiki routes are registered automatically via `nowo_wiki` routing.

After install:
1. Configure `config/packages/nowo_wiki.yaml` (`user_class`, optional ACL services).
2. Require `nowo-tech/tiptap-editor-bundle` and configure `nowo_tiptap_editor.yaml`.
3. Run Doctrine migrations for wiki tables (`{table_prefix}spaces`, `{table_prefix}pages`, `{table_prefix}page_revisions`).
4. Implement `WikiAccessCheckerInterface` and optionally `WikiTeamMembershipResolverInterface` in your app.

Manage UI: `/tools/wiki` (requires authentication on your main firewall).
